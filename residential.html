Residential Services

Whether it’s for condo or housing associations, property managers or in private residences, our team is ready to install new elevators, or service your property’s current ones. When your property needs service, our team acts fast and sends you a notification via email when the work is complete. Our up-front pricing ensures that you never get any surprises when the bill arrives.

We are in the business of moving people and understand that the safety and comfort of your tenants is the highest priority. All of our licensed technicians graduate from the four-year National Elevator Industry Education Program, so you can be sure that we perform your repairs, inspections and installations correctly and safely. We also consider floor leveling performance, door times and other factors that make your elevator more user friendly. When it’s serviced by Murphy Elevator Company, you know it’s not only in compliance; it’s adjusted for perfect performance.

If other companies have told you it can’t be done because your equipment is obsolete, give us a call. With experience in a wide breadth of elevator industry experience, we can repair and source old relays and electronics, and retool and refurbish out-of-production components.
