About Us

North Texas Elevator delivers straightforward and honest service to its customers. As a full-service provider, we offer 24-hour service, repair, modernization and installation of commercial, industrial and residential elevators. Our team is ready to work with your existing equipment — any brand, any age, any time.

Monthly Maintenance

North Texas Elevator Company’s maintenance plans include a variety of levels of service to meet the needs of your equipment. We work with you to ensure that your elevators are up to code, routinely maintained and free from issues that could cause breakdowns—all with minimal downtime. Take a look at This chart. It can help you determine which of our plans will work best for your building, but we encourage you to contact us for even more information.

Maintenance Contracts

Service Repair

As an independent elevator contractor, we have the flexibility to explore all possible repair and service options within your budget before resorting to a full equipment replacement. As part of our service agreement, we respond to breakdowns or other issues within two hours of your phone call, any time of the day for metro areas. In cases of entrapment, we can be on-location in 30 to 60 minutes. Response times will vary in more rural areas.

Our maintenance technicians carry the necessary equipment to address most routine repairs in their trucks, so many basic repairs can be done on-site at the time of inspection or during routine maintenance.

Modernisation

Elevators are a permanent fixture in your building. As with all infrastructure, elevators require upgrades with age as technology changes and equipment becomes less reliable. North Texas Elevator is here to help you determine which upgrades are necessary and whether other systems may be affected (such as fire alarm panels and HVAC).
