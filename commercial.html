Commercial Services

We’re available 24 hours a day to service, repair and inspect your elevators, ensuring they’re reliable and running safely. Our skilled technicians will keep your tests up to date, your equipment running smoothly, and most importantly, we get it done right the first time.

All work is done by our trained and licensed technicians, ensuring that your elevator never gets a state violation or has lengthy downtime. Every tech completes a four year, federally-approved National Elevator Industry Educational Program (NEIEP) apprenticeship program, so you can trust their expertise. We adhere to your site’s safety requirements, along with our own high standards.

We service all makes, models and brands of elevators: Otis, ThyssenKrupp, Kone, Dover, U.S. Elevator, Montgomery, Armor, Schindler, Fujitec, and many more.
