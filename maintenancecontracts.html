We understand the demands of industrial environments. Whether it’s a paper mill, power generation, manufacturing, chemical plant or other heavy industry, your elevators work in the most strenuous conditions possible. It’s why we’re available 24 hours a day to service, repair and inspect your elevators to ensure they’re reliable and running safely.

But we know for many companies that’s not enough – your job sited have restricted access and require compliance with safety vendors such as MICCS, PEC Safety, ISNetworld or EEI.

That’s why our technicians always comply with site-specific training requirements and background checks. We are trained for accessing elevator equipment across many industrial environments, and we understand that there will be hazards unique to your job site. We have MSHA Part 46 trained personnel that can access MSHA regulated surface mine facilities.
